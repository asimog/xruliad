Recommended Moves

  1. Position HyperMyths as attention infrastructure for token and creator launches, not just an AI video generator.
  2. Add measurable outcomes: clicks, shares, watch-through, creator conversion, or token campaign lift.
  3. Make wallet and token stories recurring and not one-shot. Monthly recap, milestone trailer, launch-day update.
  4. Ship social export artifacts around the video: thumbnail, post copy, thread draft, Telegram teaser, metadata card.
  5. Prove the source material. Show what wallet activity, token metadata, or X profile facts drove the story.
