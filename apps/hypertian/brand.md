# Hypertian Brand

Status: active

## Product
Hypertian is a self-hosted livestream ad rail for creators. It combines OBS-safe overlays, sponsor approvals, media-job review, and on-chain payment verification in one operational surface.

## Palette
Palette name: Signal Current

- `--background`: `#061015`
- `--foreground`: `#F5FBFB`
- `--primary`: `#7CE4D2`
- `--primary-foreground`: `#071114`
- `--secondary`: `#86B6FF`
- `--card-strong`: `#0C191D`
- `--muted`: `#9DB6BA`
- `--muted-foreground`: `#B6C8CA`

## Typography
- Sans: `Space Grotesk`
- Mono: `IBM Plex Mono`
- Use mono only for IDs, wallet snippets, overlay URLs, and payment references.

## Tone
Hypertian should sound calm, assured, and operational. It is a creator tool, not a casino dashboard. Messaging should emphasize ownership, approvals, reliability, and live control.

## Visual Rules
- Prefer sea-glass cyan and broadcast blue over louder crypto colors.
- Keep cards translucent and structured, not shiny.
- Use rounded geometry and pill controls to reduce perceived complexity.
- Reserve the brightest accents for state changes and primary actions.

## Avoid
- Purple hero gradients
- Fake social proof
- Overdesigned charts in the marketing UI
- Playful language that undercuts trust
