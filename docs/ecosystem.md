# Ecosystem

HyperMyths is a market network for attention, intelligence, research, simulation, and computation.

The monorepo keeps one shared platform while preserving multiple market-facing product doors:

- HyperMyths: ecosystem gateway, media engine, token scanner, wallet/X/profile intelligence.
- Hypertian: attention market for livestream ads and creator monetization.
- CancerHawk: biomedical research/data market for synthetic data quests and careful public-good research workflows.
- HyperKaon: physics simulation and compute quest market.
- Polymyths: prediction, intelligence, scenario, and narrative market layer.

The individual domains remain useful beta and product-specific entry points. HyperMyths.com can become the main hub without collapsing the other brands into one frontend.
